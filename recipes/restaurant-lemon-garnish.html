Restauraunt Lemon Garnish

Description

Sometimes your drink comes with a lemon garnish. Here's how to make the most of that.

Ingredients

  • A lemon slice is all

Steps

  1. Squeeze the slice over your beverage.
  2. Drop it in.
  3. Jab at it with your straw.
  4. Say, "Well, when life gives me a lemon slice..."
  5. (Pause artfully)
  6. Change the subject.
