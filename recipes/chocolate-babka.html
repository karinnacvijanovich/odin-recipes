Chocolate Babka

Description

The darn best babka you ever did have.

I promise.

Ingredients

  • Some eggs I think
  • Surely milk
  • Chocolate of some sort
  • Flour 4 sure

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs without getting any shells in there.
  2. If you got shells in there in the last step, fish them out with your hands.
  3. Eyeball some amount of milk.
  4. Melt the chocolate by any means possible (hairdryer, blowing really hard, quick trip to equator).
  5. Sift the entire bag of flour but then put just one spoonful in.
  6. Preheat the oven because you forgot to do that.
  7. Bake at 2 degrees F until your next birthday.
  8. Order and install another oven for the meantime.
