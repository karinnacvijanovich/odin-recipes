Peanut Butter & Fluff

Description

I used to really like these back in the day. Don't think I could stomach one now, but you go ahead.

Ingredients

  • Salted Peanuts
  • Unsalted Butter
  • the fluffiest Fluff you can get your hands on
  • Oh and bread

Steps

  1. Put the peanuts in a jar.
  2. Stare off into middle distance; wonder what is the closest you've ever been to Gwyneth Paltrow, physically and/or spiritually.
  3. Take the peanuts out of the jar and press them firmly into the stick of butter.
  4. Start throwing fluff at the peanut-butterstick-sculpture as haphazardly as possible.
  5. Slice your bread and gingerly place it atop your fluffy masterpiece.
  6. Remark, "Well isn't this the best thing since sliced bread?" to the echoes of an empty room.
